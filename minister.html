About Ambassador
Yusuf Tuggar

Ambassador Yusuf Maitama Tuggar, born on March 12, 1967, in North-Eastern State, Nigeria, is a seasoned diplomat and politician currently serving as Nigeria's Minister of Foreign Affairs under President Bola Ahmed Tinubu's administration.

Since August 21, 2023, he has steered Nigeria's foreign policy through the innovative "4D Doctrine" — Demography, Development, Diaspora, and Democracy — working to elevate Nigeria's regional influence, economic partnerships, cultural heritage and citizen protection worldwide.

A Chevening Scholar and graduate of the University of Cambridge, Ambassador Tuggar brings over 25 years of experience spanning the oil and gas industry, political risk consulting, journalism, legislative service and senior diplomatic representation.

As Ambassador to Germany from 2017 to 2023, he deepened bilateral ties between Nigeria and Germany across trade, investment, development cooperation and cultural exchange, leaving a lasting legacy of strengthened relations between the two nations.
