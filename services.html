Document Authentication

Authenticate Your Official Documents

The Ministry authenticates a range of public documents for use abroad, confirming their validity to foreign governments and institutions. Begin any request through the Paperless Service Portal.

Birth Certificate Authentication

The Ministry authenticates Nigerian birth certificates issued by the National Population Commission (NPC) for use in visa applications, overseas education, employment and residency purposes.

Required Documents

  • Original birth certificate issued by the NPC
  • National Identity card / NIN slip of the applicant
  • Completed application form (available at the Ministry)
  • Two (2) recent passport photographs
  • Evidence of payment of prescribed fee
  • Letter of purpose (stating where the document will be used)

How to Apply

1
Create an account on the Paperless Service Portal.
2
Upload scanned copies of all required documents.
3
Pay the prescribed fee online via Remita.
4
Visit the Ministry's Consular counter with originals for verification.
5
Collect your authenticated document within 5–7 working days.
Start Application

Processing time: 5–7 working days. Bring original documents to the Ministry.

Child Adoption Document Authentication

Authentication of adoption papers is required for international adoption processes, visa applications for adopted children and legal recognition of adoptions by foreign authorities.

Required Documents

  • Court adoption order / final adoption decree
  • Birth certificate of the adopted child
  • Valid ID of the adopting parent(s)
  • Completed application form
  • Two (2) passport photographs
  • Payment receipt of prescribed fee
  • Home study report (for international adoptions)

How to Apply

1
Obtain a certified true copy of the court adoption order.
2
Submit all documents via the Paperless Service Portal.
3
Pay the prescribed fee online via Remita.
4
Attend a verification appointment at the Consular Department.
5
Collect authenticated documents within 7–10 working days.
Start Application

Adoption cases may require additional documentation. Contact the Consular Department for guidance.

End User Certificates (EUC)

End User Certificates are issued by the Ministry to certify the identity, legitimacy and intended end-use of Nigerian entities importing controlled goods, including defence and dual-use equipment.

Required Documents

  • Formal application letter on company letterhead
  • Supplier's pro-forma invoice or contract
  • CAC certificate and TIN of the importing entity
  • Ministerial or regulatory approval (where applicable)
  • Evidence of payment of EUC processing fee
  • Completed EUC application form

How to Apply

1
Submit a formal application to the Consular & Legal Department.
2
Attach all required documents, including CAC certificate and invoice.
3
Pay the EUC processing fee via the Remita platform.
4
Await Ministry review and inter-agency verification (if applicable).
5
Collect issued EUC within 10–14 working days.
Start Application

EUC applications are subject to security vetting. Processing time may vary.

Marriage Certificate Authentication

The Ministry authenticates Nigerian marriage certificates for use in spousal visa applications, name change processes, inheritance claims and other legal purposes in foreign countries.

Required Documents

  • Original marriage certificate (court, church or customary)
  • Valid IDs of both spouses
  • Two (2) passport photographs of each spouse
  • Completed application form
  • Evidence of payment of prescribed fee
  • Letter of purpose (country and reason for authentication)

How to Apply

1
Submit your application via the Paperless Service Portal.
2
Upload clear scans of the marriage certificate and IDs.
3
Pay the prescribed fee online via Remita.
4
Visit the Ministry's Consular counter with originals for stamping.
5
Collect your authenticated certificate within 5–7 working days.
Start Application

Both spouses must be present or provide notarised consent if one is absent.

Diplomatic

Diplomatic Passport

Nigerian diplomats and other senior government officials may be approved by the President. Applications require:

  • Letter of application / introduction from the applicant's organisation
  • Two (2) passport photographs
  • Last promotion letter or letter of appointment
  • Photocopy of a valid official identity card
  • Evidence of age
  • Certificate of return from INEC (for eligible political office holders)
