Passports

The Nigerian Passport

All citizens of Nigeria — by birth, descent, adoption, registration or naturalisation — are eligible for a Nigerian passport, provided they satisfy all the conditions prescribed by law.

Eligibility

  • Commissioners (members of the State Executive Council), Special Assistants and Special Advisers
  • Senior officials of the rank of Deputy Director and above in the Federal Civil Service
  • Senior officials of the rank of Director and above in the State Civil Service

  • Chairmen, Deputy Chairmen and Secretaries of Local Government Councils

Diplomatic

Diplomatic Passport

Nigerian diplomats and other senior government officials may be approved by the President. Applications require:

  • Letter of application / introduction from the applicant's organisation
  • Two (2) passport photographs
  • Last promotion letter or letter of appointment
  • Photocopy of a valid official identity card
  • Evidence of age
  • Certificate of return from INEC (for eligible political office holders)
