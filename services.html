Services

Document Authentication

Authenticate Your Official Documents

The Ministry authenticates a range of public documents for use abroad, confirming their validity to foreign governments and institutions. Begin any request through the Paperless Service Portal.

Birth Certificate

Authentication of Nigerian birth certificates for visa, study, employment and residency purposes overseas.

Start Request

Child Adoption Documents

Verification and authentication of adoption papers in line with national and international legal requirements.

Start Request

End User Certificates

Processing of End User Certificates for the lawful importation of controlled goods and equipment.

Start Request

Marriage Certificates

Authentication of marriage certificates for spousal visas, name changes and legal recognition abroad.

Visa & Consular

Consular & Travel Services

Our missions provide visas to foreign nationals travelling to Nigeria and a full range of consular support to Nigerians abroad — from passport renewals and emergency travel certificates to citizen welfare and document legalisation.

Visas — apply for tourist, business, official and transit visas through accredited Nigerian missions.

Consular Services — passport services, attestations, citizen assistance and diaspora support worldwide.
