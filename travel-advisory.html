Travel Advisory

Essential guidance for safe and informed travel — for Nigerians abroad and visitors to Nigeria.

Overview

Travel Advisory

Whether you're travelling for business, tourism or relocation, our up-to-date travel advisories provide essential information on visa policies, security alerts, health regulations and diplomatic recommendations for Nigerian travellers worldwide.

Get Started

Going abroad

For Nigerians Travelling Abroad

Requirements

  • Valid passport (minimum 6-month validity)
  • Visa or entry permit for the destination country
  • Health insurance
  • Register with the nearest Nigerian Embassy

Safety Tips

  • Obey local laws
  • Keep copies of your documents
  • Avoid late-night travel
  • Stay alert in public spaces

In an emergency, contact the nearest Nigerian Embassy or Consulate.

Coming to Nigeria

For Foreigners Visiting Nigeria

Requirements

  • Valid passport
  • Nigerian visa (unless visa-exempt)
  • Yellow fever card
  • Return ticket or travel itinerary

Security Notice

Avoid non-essential travel to high-risk areas.

Entry Advice

  • Respect Nigerian laws
  • Use verified transport
  • Avoid cash-heavy transactions
