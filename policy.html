The 4D Foreign Policy

Nigeria's foreign policy is anchored on the 4D framework — Democracy & Good Governance, Development, Demography, and Diaspora — guiding the nation's diplomatic engagement and its pursuit of national interest on the global stage.

Democracy & Good Governance

Promoting democratic values, the rule of law and stability across the region and beyond.

Development

Advancing economic diplomacy, trade, investment and partnerships that drive national prosperity.

Demography

Harnessing Nigeria's youthful population as a force for innovation, talent and global influence.

Diaspora

Engaging and protecting Nigerians abroad and channelling diaspora capacity toward national growth.

Activities

Through high-level visits, bilateral and multilateral engagements, economic diplomacy missions and diaspora outreach, the Ministry translates the 4D framework into concrete action — deepening Nigeria's partnerships and advancing its strategic priorities worldwide.
