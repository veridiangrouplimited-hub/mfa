About Ministry of Foreign Affairs

Who We Are Mandate Mission Vision Core Values Leadership

Who We Are

As the principal arm of Nigeria's foreign engagement, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs leads the nation's diplomatic missions and international relations. We are driven by a deep commitment to advancing Nigeria's global interests, promoting peace and cooperation, and representing the values and aspirations of our people. Grounded in professionalism, service, and strategic policy-making, we serve as the trusted bridge between Nigeria and the rest of the world.

Mandate

The Ministry is charged with the formulation, articulation and implementation of Nigeria's foreign policy. It coordinates the country's relations with other states and international organisations, protects Nigerian citizens and interests abroad, and projects the nation's image and values on the global stage.

Mission

To promote and protect Nigeria's national interests in the international arena, foster friendly relations and cooperation with other nations, and provide responsive, citizen-centred consular services to Nigerians at home and in the diaspora.

Vision

To be a world-class foreign service that effectively advances Nigeria's strategic interests, strengthens its standing as a leader in Africa, and contributes meaningfully to global peace, security and development.

Core Values

Professionalism

Upholding the highest standards of diplomatic conduct, competence and integrity in service to the nation.

Patriotism

Placing Nigeria's national interest at the heart of every engagement and decision we make abroad.

Service

Delivering timely, accessible and citizen-centred support to Nigerians at home and in the diaspora.

Partnership

Building enduring relationships founded on mutual respect, cooperation and shared prosperity.

Leadership

The Ministry is led by the Honourable Minister of Foreign Affairs, supported by the Minister of State, the Permanent Secretary, Department Directors and Heads of Nigeria's Permanent Missions across the world. Together they steer the nation's diplomatic agenda and oversee its global network of embassies, high commissions and consulates.
